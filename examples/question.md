# Example: Question

## Purpose

The question example tests whether Energy Theory is a question-finder rather than an answer-machine.

## Statement

A question is a bounded unresolved energy-state.

Energy Theory answers:

```text
What questions do I ask?
```

It does so by finding shared question-forms among domains.

## Structure

A question defines boundary.

The boundary defines a question-simplex.

The question-simplex holds energy in a state of unresolved being.

```text
question = bounded unresolved energy-state
```

## Domain restraint

Questions may appear in language, but questions are not owned by linguistics.

A system state, sensor anomaly, unsatisfied proof, broken build, social tension, thermodynamic imbalance, or unfilled cup-space may all generate question-form when bounded unresolved energy-state becomes registrable.

## Guardrail

Do not say:

```text
Energy Theory gives answers first.
```

Say:

```text
Energy Theory locates the question that makes transformation legible.
```
