# Test Spec: Theorem 04 — Observer Role by Registration

## Claim

Observer is a role performed by a simplex when it registers sequential tensor alteration.

Observer is not a required kind of being.

Observer does not necessarily mean mind.

## Procedure

1. Define a simplex `S`.
2. Define a sequence of tensor alterations `T0 -> T1 -> T2`.
3. Case A: place `S` in the tensor without recording the sequence.
4. Confirm `S` is not playing observer role.
5. Case B: allow `S` to preserve, express, or transmit the sequence.
6. Confirm `S` is playing observer role.

## Passing condition

A simplex becomes observer only when it performs registration.

Mere presence in a tensor is not observer role.

## Failing condition

The model treats any present simplex as observer.

The model requires consciousness for observer role.

## Minimal assertion

```text
simplex + registration = observer role
simplex without registration != observer role
```
